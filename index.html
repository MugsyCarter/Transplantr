Who

Every year, approximately 14% of Americans move. Whether it be to another part of their city or to another state entirely, over 40 million Americans are scouring the internet and other resources to find the information and tools they need to relocate.

What

Transplantr was established in 2016 with a mission to provide Americans with the information they need to plan their move. While a plethora of websites can provide this information, Transplantr serves to be an all-in-one resource.

Why

Zillow, a popular real estate website, provides users with property listings in a given area but does not offer the average salary and cost of living in that area. BestPlaces is a website that helps users compare the average cost of living in their current city to their provided destination. While both tools are userful, they fail to take into account the average housing prices in relation to the cost of living in that area. As a result, Transplantr combines this information with the added feature of comparing this information to the national average to give the user a comprehensive side-by-side comparison.
